🆕What's New for Our Trip

Park updates & changes arriving between now and January 2027

🏰

Big Thunder Mountain — Brand New Track

Reopening spring 2026 with a completely replaced track and new glowing Rainbow Caverns story elements. Veterans: it'll feel like a different ride (in a good way). First-timers: you're getting it at peak condition.

🚀

Buzz Lightyear — Updated & Upgraded

Buzz Lightyear's Space Ranger Spin reopens spring 2026 with new handheld blasters, updated ride vehicles, and interactive targets. The classic is back and better.

⚠️

Rivers of America — Gone

The Rivers of America has been permanently filled in to make way for a future Cars/Piston Peak land. Tom Sawyer Island and the Liberty Square Riverboat are no longer there. Veterans will notice.

🌍

Soarin' — New Film: Across America

A brand new Soarin' film debuts May 26, 2026 celebrating the US 250th anniversary — a flight over American landmarks. No more California or globe versions. A fresh experience for veterans and first-timers alike.

🎬

Rock 'n' Roller Coaster — Now Starring The Muppets

The coaster itself is unchanged (same layout, same speed), but it's been rethemed from Aerosmith to a Muppet Studio chaos adventure arriving in 2026. Veterans will notice; first-timers will just have a blast.

🦁

Animal Kingdom — Active Construction Zone

The entire DinoLand U.S.A. area is being demolished and replaced by Tropical Americas (Encanto + Indiana Jones rides), but that new land won't open until late 2027 at the earliest. Expect the area to be walled off during our visit. Plan accordingly — Animal Kingdom may have fewer attractions than you remember.

🌹Where to Find Belle

Two reliable spots, plus one common misconception to avoid.

🇫🇷

Best bet: France Pavilion at EPCOT

Belle has a dedicated walk-up meet at the France Pavilion in World Showcase — she stands in the shade by the water's edge. Times are posted in the My Disney Experience app each day. Typical wait 20–35 min; meet itself ~8 min. Best windows: first 30 min after rope drop or the last hour before park close. Avoid 11 AM – 2 PM. Convenient bonus: France is already on the EPCOT day for Festival of the Arts and the crepes/Les Halles stop.

📖

Bonus: Enchanted Tales with Belle (MK Fantasyland)

An interactive 20-minute show where you walk through the wardrobe into the Beast's library and Belle "tells" the story. Catch: only guests who volunteer for a role in the show get a photo with Belle at the end — so put your hand up. The attraction was dark 2020–2023 but has been operating since reopening. Worth verifying it's still running closer to the trip.

⚠️

Don't count on these

Be Our Guest is the Beauty and the Beast restaurant, but only Beast appears — Belle is not there. Cinderella's Royal Table sometimes rotates Belle in, but the regulars are Cinderella, Aurora, Ariel, Snow White, and Jasmine. If meeting Belle matters, plan around the EPCOT spot, not these.

🌤️January at Disney World
🧥

Weather: Cool & Comfortable

January is one of the best months to visit — crowds are lower and temps are mild. Expect highs around 70°F (21°C) and lows around 50°F (10°C). Pack a light jacket for evenings and mornings.

👥

Crowd Levels: Low Season

After the holiday rush, January 10–17 is typically one of the quietest stretches of the year. Shorter lines, easier dining reservations, and a more relaxed pace.

🎄

Possible Lingering Decor

Disney usually keeps holiday decorations up through early January. We may catch the tail end of the Christmas magic — a bonus!

📱Must-Know Apps & Planning
📱

My Disney Experience App

Download it before you go. This is your command center: mobile ordering, wait times, Lightning Lane purchases, park maps, and more. Get the app →

⚡

Lightning Lane Strategy

Lightning Lane Premier Pass covers the most popular rides (TRON, Guardians, Rise of the Resistance, Flight of Passage). Purchases open at 7 AM — resort guests get first access. Worth it for the big headliners.

🌅

Early Theme Park Entry

As Wilderness Lodge guests, we get 30 minutes of Early Entry every day before parks open to the public. Use this to hit the most popular rides with minimal waits.

🍔

Mobile Ordering = No Lines

Order food through the MDE app at quick-service restaurants — skip the ordering queue entirely. Available at most counter-service spots across all parks.

🏕️Wilderness Lodge Perks
⛵

Boat to Magic Kingdom

One of the best perks — a scenic ferry boat runs directly from Wilderness Lodge to Magic Kingdom. Skip the monorail crowds.

🏊

Resort Amenities

The lodge has a stunning Pacific Northwest–themed lobby, hot springs pool, and access to Bay Lake. Perfect for a resort day between park days.

🚌

Transportation

Disney buses run to all four parks and Disney Springs. EPCOT and Hollywood Studios can also be reached via the Skyliner gondola from other resorts (short bus hop).

🎒Packing Tips for January
👟

Comfortable Shoes Are Non-Negotiable

Expect 10–15 miles of walking per park day. Break in new shoes before the trip — blisters are the #1 trip killer.

🧴

Still Need Sunscreen

Florida sun is strong even in January. SPF 30+ is still worth it, especially for outdoor queues and parades.

🎒

Small Backpack Essentials

Light jacket or hoodie for evenings, portable phone charger, reusable water bottle (free ice water at any quick-service counter!), light snacks.

💳

Link Cards to MDE App

Link your credit card to My Disney Experience for tap-to-pay at any shop or restaurant. Much faster than fumbling for your wallet.

🗺️Travel Details
✈️Getting There
🛬

Orlando International Airport (MCO)

The closest airport — about 25 miles from Wilderness Lodge. Disney's Magical Express no longer operates; plan for rideshare (Uber/Lyft), rental car, or Mears Connect shuttle.

🚗

Ground Transport Options

Uber/Lyft: ~$35–50 from MCO · Mears Connect shared shuttle: ~$16–20/person · Rental car: free self-parking at resort included for guests.
